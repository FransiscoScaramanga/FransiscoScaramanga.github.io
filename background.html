Background Information

Born in Sydney, I enjoyed an international upbringing, including schooling in SE Asia and the UK. After high school, I studied Pharmacy as it seemed a sensible choice and I had an interest in Science. For several years after graduation I worked as a Pharmacist, predominantly freelance. During this time, I met my wife and we started a family. Having an interest in the area and wanting to gain a more thorough understanding of how the world works, I studied for and gained a masters degree in international business law when the opportunity to do so presented itself. In 2020, our family decided to ‘Brexit’ and moved to Brisbane to see what life back in Australia has to offer.
