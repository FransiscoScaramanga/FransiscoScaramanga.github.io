Interest in IT

I couldn’t say when my interest in IT started. I’ve always been interested in problem solving and developments in technology but haven’t found the time to explore this. Often I have found myself wondering whether I could have designed an application I work with better or whether I could create something that would do this or that at work. A few times I have taken these things a bit further but nothing has gone too far, I feel that if I develop myself I’ll be able to carry these and other projects further.

I decided to study Intro to IT at RMIT after enjoying Intro to Programming. I originally undertook this as I had no experience in programming and didn’t know whether it was something I would enjoy. Turns out that I did and so a natural progression was to continue on to IT. I’m hoping to gain a thorough understanding of the digital world. I feel I have a useful understanding of healthcare and law and would like to say the same of IT.

Ideal Job

Software Developer - Medical Computing

One of the reasons that I have an interest in studying IT is that I have had several ideas that I would like to develop and I have found that, although I’ve been told the ideas aren’t difficult to develop, I have struggled to find people who are actually able to deliver what I have asked in a professional manner and at a fair price. Ideally, I would like to fill that void for others, drawing on my experience as a healthcare professional, be that as part of a larger organisation or otherwise. Of course, I expect that over the course of my studies, I may develop other interests and my ideal role could change accordingly.

The position advertised is that of a software developer, working with image analysis, visualization and simulation in the medical field. This job appeals to me as it seems a way of applying a skills that I am developing to a field that I am more familiar with. The position advertised appears to be one that has scope for a great deal of development, which I consider important as a lack of this is one of the factors behind me attempting a career switch.

The skills required include a degree in a field related to computer science, software development experience and a GitHub portfolio, all things that I am currently working towards.
